Bio

Ayoub is an AI researcher currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Computer Science, specializing in multimodel Ai and Medical Ai at Shanghai Jiaotong University. His work has led to novel deep learning applications in healthcare, particularly for treatment management and brain aging. He is the author of the book “A Stroke Guide: Understanding, Factors, and Lifestyle Management”. He serves as Young Emerging Editor for the Journal of Medicine Advances, Young Editor for Biomedical Engineering Communications, and Editor for the Journal of Psychological Medicine and Deep Science Publishing. He is also a reviewer for leading journals and conferences including the IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics and Computers in Biology and Medicine, Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, Information Processing and Maangement. With over a dozen peer-reviewed publications in top-tier journals, around 400 citations, an h-index of 15, and several academic awards—including first prize at Technovanza—Ayoub continues to contribute actively to the advancement of AI in biomedical research.
